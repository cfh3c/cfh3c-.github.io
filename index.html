I am currently a third year Ph.D student in Cognitive Science Department of Xiamen University. My advisor is Prof. Rongrong Ji. I received the B.S. Degree in Cognitive Science and Technology in 2014 from Xiamen University. I obtained the M.S.-Ph.D qualification and finished my M.S. in 2016 in Xiamen University.

My research interests are in Computer vision, Multimedia and Machine learning, especially in Vision-language content understanding, Visual-textual structured representation, and Multimodal sentiment analysis.
