I am currently a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Computer Science of the University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Wenping Wang. Before that, I received the PH.D. Degree from the Department of Artificial Intelligence of Xiamen University in 2020, supervised by Prof. Rongrong Ji in Media Analysis and Computing Lab (MAC). I joint the Department of Computer Science of National University of Singapore in the same year, working with A/Prof. Angela Yao. Earlier, I received the B.S. Degree from the Department of Cognitive Science and Technology (now renamed as Department of Artificial Intelligence) of Xiamen University in 2014. I obtained the M.S.-Ph.D. qualification and finished my M.S. career in Xiamen University in 2016.

My research interests are on computer vision, multimedia, natural language processing, and machine learning, especially on vision-language content understanding, multi-modal structured semantic modeling, diverse semantic inference, causal semantic inference, as well as their applications, including image/video captioning, multimedia sentiment analysis, image-sentence retrieval, human-computer interaction, sentence grounding in video, and medical diagnostic report analysis.
